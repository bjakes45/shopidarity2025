🛍️ Shopidarity FAQ

❓ What is Shopidarity?
Shopidarity is a solidarity-based shopping tool. You can find and share deals, then team up with others for bulk buying — saving money and building community power.
🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Who is Shopidarity for?

Shopidarity is designed for everyday people who want to align their spending with their values while saving money. Here are just a few types of users who benefit from Shopidarity:

Who What They’re Looking For How Shopidarity Helps
🧾 Budget-Conscious Shoppers Ways to save money on everyday purchases Join collective carts and share deals to unlock group discounts
🌱 Ethical Consumers Support brands that match their values Favorite ethical products and discover value-aligned product groups
📦 Local Coordinators Ways to organize community buying or pickups Use product groups to find neighbors and collaborate on purchases
🎯 Deal Sharers Enjoy curating and sharing discounts with others Contribute to product groups by posting timely deals
🔁 How does it work?
  1. Scan a barcode - Add a new product if it isn't already in our library
  2. Rate, favorite and comment on the products you buy everyday.
  3. Add a deal – via link, receipt, API, or manually
  4. Find like-minded shoppers and form product groups
  5. Host or join a Collective Cart to bulk buy together
➕ Why should I add products?
Adding products helps grow our shared library, making it easier for everyone to find relevant deals and build product groups. By contributing products you care about, you’re seeding a resource that benefits shoppers looking for the same items, enabling better deals, stronger communities, and more collective buying power.
🧾 How do I add a deal?
Deals let people know WHERE to find the products they like and HOW MUCH they cost.
You will be able to submit a deal in four ways:
  • Enter it manually (current)
  • Paste a product link (current but unreliable)
  • Upload a receipt photo (in development)
  • Sync with a proof-of-purchase API (in development)
🛒 What is a Collective Cart?
A group order where people buy together to save money. Hosts set a quantity goal and invite others. Once the goal is reached, one big order is placed.
📍 What are product groups?
Join a local group focused on a specific product. Like like a fan club, but for your favorite products. Get alerts for deals and Collective Carts in your area, and coordinate pickups together.
🌀 Is this a relaunch?
Yes! Shopidarity began over a decade ago as an idea ahead of its time. Back then, the tools and user behaviors needed for collective shopping weren’t quite in place. Our team spent that time learning about community organizing and mutual aid best practices. This relaunch brings the concept into today’s contex. We are combining barcode scanning, social shopping habits, and a renewed interest in community-powered savings. The spirit is the same: use shared purchasing power to reward ethical brands and save money together.
🔐 Is my data safe?
Yes. All API features are opt-in. You choose what to share, and we never sell your data.
