🛍️ Shopidarity FAQ

❓ What is Shopidarity?
Shopidarity is a solidarity-based shopping tool. You can find and share deals, then team up with others for bulk buying — saving money and building community power.
🔁 How does it work?
  1. Scan a barcode
  2. Add a new product (if not found)
  3. Add a deal – via link, receipt, API, or manually
  4. Host or join a collective cart to bulk buy together
🧾 How do I add a deal?
You can submit a deal in four ways:
  • Paste a product link
  • Upload a receipt photo
  • Sync with a proof-of-purchase API (coming soon)
  • Enter it manually
🛒 What is a collective cart?
A group order where people buy together to save money. Hosts set a quantity goal and invite others. Once the goal is reached, one big order is placed.
📍 What are groups?
Join a local group focused on a specific product (like tofu or rice). Get alerts for deals and carts in your area, and coordinate pickups together.
🔐 Is my data safe?
Yes. All API features are opt-in. You choose what to share, and we never sell your data.
⚙️ Coming soon:
  • Auto alerts for tracked products
  • Trust scores for cart hosts and deal contributors
  • Delivery coordination tools for collective carts
